LJScripts Free URL Rotator Setup Instructions

  1. Upload the index.php, url_db and configuration.php files to any folder on your site.

  2. CHMOD the above files to:

    url_db - 0777 (rwxrwxrwx)
    configuration.php - 0777 (rwxrwxrwx)
    index.php - 0755 (rwxr-xr-x)

  3. Go to:

    http://www.your-site.com/rotator_folder/?admin=edadmin - change password/username
    http://www.your-site.com/rotator_folder/?admin=edurls - add/edit URLs

    http://www.your-site.com/rotator_folder/ - Your rotator

    First time login details:

    Username: admin_username
    Password: password

    NOTE: The admin username has an underscore ( _ ) character. If you are having problems logging in after the install, copy and paste the above login details into the login form.
